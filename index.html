Our Products

ManyCents Resources specializes in the supply of premium quality coal products for industrial and energy applications worldwide.

Anthracite Coal

Premium high-rank anthracite with low ash content (<15%), ideal for industrial base load and specialized applications. Available in sized and unsized products.

Applications: Industrial heating, metallurgy, filtration

Lean Coal

Quality lean coal products meeting international specifications for various industrial and energy generation applications.

Applications: Power generation, cement production, industrial processes

Major Projects

Magomba Anthracite Coal Mine

Located in Eswatini, the Magomba Coal Project commenced operations in February 2025. This flagship project is positioned as a strategic, reliable supplier of premium anthracite to the European market. Built on a robust geological foundation with approximately 12.8 million tonnes of measured resources, Magomba exemplifies our commitment to quality and reliability.

Key Advantages

Flexible Processing

Campaign washing with Dense Medium Cyclone plant allows us to wash ROM coal to exact customer specifications.

Maputo Corridor Access

Optimal positioning near Mpaka rail siding with only ~120km to Maputo port, providing significant competitive advantage.

Dual Port Strategy

Matola Coal Terminal for high-volume fines and Maputo Dry Bulk quays for sized anthracite to minimize breakage.

Market Responsive

Ability to immediately adjust product mix and operating parameters to maximize efficiency and meet market demands.
